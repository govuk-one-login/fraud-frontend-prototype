October 2023

Version 1-0-0

This is the first design story for the Shared Signals UI prototype.

The main features of this initial prototype include:

  • Upload information
  • View previous uploaded information

Upload information

Initial upload functionality includes upload by file, one by one by a form.

View previous uploaded information

This initial prototype covers the unhappy path of showing unsuccessful submissions. This is handled with a list of submissions and links to view submission details.

Areas to look at next

Look to iterate the upload functionality after usability testing, and understanding of impact wider discussions.

Issues flagged

Due to the complexity of the language used to describe RISC events, I've flagged there could be a need for a Content Designer, or Technical Writer.

Other design activities for a content designer would be to input in the flow and hierachy of the journey and naming of the sservice.

Also we are still understanding the RP requirements, so initially the designs that we will be testing, are focused on the core functionality - initially Uploading information and Downloading information.
